The Challenge

MTF Finance planned a big-splash campaign to launch their new brand platform featuring 'Guardian Guy'. The objective of the campaign was to help raise brand awareness in a crowded, competitive space and support the launch of the new personal loan product nationwide.

Calibrate was called upon to develop the strategy, media plan, landing pages and creative design for digital channels. We were then tasked with implementing and optimising all media campaign throughout the campaign period and beyond.

The Solution

Unifying Analytics & Reporting

Calibrate worked with MTF Finance to set clear measures for success across the customer journey. These were built into a real-time marketing dashboard that all stakeholders could access. We also merged offline business data into the report to track actual business results - not just online leads.

Media planning and management

Calibrate took responsibility for driving the digital advertising channels, activating and converting traffic from paid social, search, display and video.

Dedicated campaign landing pages

Calibrate built landing pages to support the digital campaigns, working with agency partners to ensure consistency of the brand and messaging throughout the customer journey. The landing pages included multiple variants which were tested to understand the most effective approach for converting visitors.

The Results

The MTF Finance annual report celebrated the “Best Sales Performance” ever with sales up 11% to $670 million - largely due to a 41% increase in online loan applications year on year.

Calibrate's use of offline business data, with clear sight of online conversions as an indicator, helped guide stakeholders at MTF Finance through process of test-learn-optimise.

MTF Finance were able to act on live campaign results, and when customer attraction and conversion hit its optimal point, they were able to scale their campaigns and maximise the return on their investment.

The Guardian Guy brand platform has been well received with brand metrics and search demand increasing for MTF Finance.
